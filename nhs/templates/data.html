Where does the data come from?

All of the data presented on Open Prescribing comes from publically available sources - let's break that down a little...

Prescribing Data

The prescribing data comes from the NHS Information Centre GP prescribing activity.

Geolocation Data

The geolocation data comes from the Ordnance Survey Code-Point Open data, using the mySociety Mapit project.

The CCG boundaries come from the list of proposed CCGs available here

Practice Metadata

Practice dispensing status comes from the NHS Business Services Authority Monthly report

Demographic data for GP surgeries is sourced from the NHS IC Indicators Portal GP Practice Data
