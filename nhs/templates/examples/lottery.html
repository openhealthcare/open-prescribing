Erlotinib

Erlotinib is a treatment for lung cancer. It is an epidermal growth factor receptor inhibitor. It is recommended by NICE as an alternative treatment for patients with non-small-cell lung cancer (NSCLC) who have already tried one chemotherapy regimen but it has not worked. It is expensive and rarely used.
